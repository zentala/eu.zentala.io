Digital Integration

Building a unified European digital government infrastructure to streamline administration and provide pan-European digital services

Digital Government Vision

Digital Integration proposes building a unified European digital government infrastructure instead of 27 separate solutions. By developing software collectively while allowing each nation to host and customize implementations, we can achieve economies of scale and true administrative integration across the EU.

The Digital Government Revolution

Envisioning a future where every citizen, police officer, and judge interacts with the state through seamless digital systems. The government of tomorrow will be fully digital, paperless, and accessible through user-friendly interfaces.

Building a Unified Digital Europe

Instead of creating a digital Tower of Babel with each EU country digitalizing separately, let's craft a shared vision for e-government applications. By building together, we can achieve economies of scale and true digital integration across the EU.

Collaborative Development, Individual Implementation

We can develop software collectively as EU countries, sharing an open-source approach, while allowing each nation to host and customize for its citizens. This ensures both unity in development and flexibility in implementation.

European Digital-First Governance

Creating a comprehensive digital system that prioritizes online interactions, making government services more efficient and accessible.

Governance Innovations

Code as Law

Recognizing computer code as a valid format for expressing and implementing laws, paving the way for more precise and automated governance.

Digital Direct Democracy

Leveraging digital systems to enable more direct forms of democracy, increasing citizen participation in decision-making processes.

European Blockchain Archives

Implementing blockchain technology to ensure the immutability and full observability of public records, eliminating the need for physical archives.

Council of Software Architects

Establishing a 'parliament' of programmers and developers to debate and shape the future of European software and its standardization for the EU.

European Digital Health Care

Digital Predictive Health Care

Launching a groundbreaking European research project to build a predictive medicine institute. Leveraging big data from across the EU to train AI models for early disease detection, revolutionizing preventive care for all Europeans.

AI-Powered Diagnostics

Implementing cutting-edge AI systems to assist doctors in analyzing health data, considering past medical records and genetic information for more accurate diagnoses.

Personalized Medicine

Harnessing the power of big data and genetics to provide tailored, high-quality healthcare solutions for every EU citizen at minimal marginal cost.

Unified European Health Database

Creating a secure, anonymized health database spanning the EU to drive research, improve treatments, and enable rapid responses to health crises.

European eLearning System

The Future of Education is Digital

Embracing eLearning as the primary mode of education delivery, preparing European citizens for a digital-first world.

Lifelong Learning

Creating a framework for continuous learning throughout all life stages, enabling career transitions and skill upgrades for all Europeans.

AI-Powered Personalized Learning

Utilizing artificial intelligence to tailor educational content and pace to individual learners, maximizing educational outcomes.

Financial Reforms

Near-Zero Transaction Costs

Eliminating excessive fees for digital money transfers, treating financial transactions as a public service rather than a profit center for banks.

Digital Payment Zone

Creating a unified payment system across all 27 EU countries, facilitating seamless transactions regardless of local currency.

Automated Taxation

Designing a system where businesses can issue invoices and receive payments through a state-provided platform, with taxes automatically calculated and deducted.

Flexible Taxation for the Gig Economy

Implementing an adaptable tax system that accommodates the diverse and fluid nature of employment in the AI era.

Digital Receipts

Automatically generating digital receipts for all card payments, streamlining record-keeping for both consumers and businesses.

Benefits

For Citizens

  • • Streamlined access to government services
  • • Reduced bureaucracy and paperwork
  • • Better healthcare through data-driven insights
  • • Enhanced educational opportunities
  • • Simplified financial transactions

For Businesses

  • • Simplified compliance and regulatory procedures
  • • Reduced administrative costs
  • • Seamless cross-border operations
  • • Digital payment infrastructure
  • • Access to pan-European markets

For Governments

  • • Economies of scale in software development
  • • Reduced administrative costs
  • • Improved service delivery
  • • Better data collection and analysis
  • • Enhanced policy implementation

For European Integration

  • • Standardized digital infrastructure
  • • Enhanced cross-border collaboration
  • • Shared resources and expertise
  • • Collective problem-solving
  • • Digital foundation for further integration
