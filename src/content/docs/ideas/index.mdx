# European Integration Vision

This is a collection of interrelated ideas aimed at strengthening European integration through practical, mutually beneficial reforms. Rather than focusing on institutional changes, this vision centers on creating the shared infrastructure, language, and digital systems that would naturally bring Europeans closer together.

## Guiding Principles

<div class="my-8 p-6 bg-gray-50 dark:bg-gray-800 rounded-lg shadow-md">
  <div class="grid grid-cols-1 md:grid-cols-2 gap-6">
    <div class="flex flex-col">
      <div class="flex items-center mb-2">
        <div class="w-8 h-8 flex-shrink-0 flex items-center justify-center bg-blue-500 rounded-full mr-3">
          <span class="text-white font-bold">1</span>
        </div>
        <h3 class="text-lg font-bold">Seeking Mutual Benefit</h3>
      </div>
      <p class="ml-11">Focus on reforms that provide clear advantages to all member states, fostering a spirit of collective progress rather than zero-sum competition.</p>
    </div>
    
    <div class="flex flex-col">
      <div class="flex items-center mb-2">
        <div class="w-8 h-8 flex-shrink-0 flex items-center justify-center bg-blue-500 rounded-full mr-3">
          <span class="text-white font-bold">2</span>
        </div>
        <h3 class="text-lg font-bold">Leveraging Economies of Scale</h3>
      </div>
      <p class="ml-11">Develop solutions collectively that would be too expensive or complex for individual nations to create independently.</p>
    </div>
    
    <div class="flex flex-col">
      <div class="flex items-center mb-2">
        <div class="w-8 h-8 flex-shrink-0 flex items-center justify-center bg-blue-500 rounded-full mr-3">
          <span class="text-white font-bold">3</span>
        </div>
        <h3 class="text-lg font-bold">Building a Common Culture</h3>
      </div>
      <p class="ml-11">Foster a European identity alongside national cultures through shared systems, language, and infrastructure.</p>
    </div>
    
    <div class="flex flex-col">
      <div class="flex items-center mb-2">
        <div class="w-8 h-8 flex-shrink-0 flex items-center justify-center bg-blue-500 rounded-full mr-3">
          <span class="text-white font-bold">4</span>
        </div>
        <h3 class="text-lg font-bold">Improving Quality of Life</h3>
      </div>
      <p class="ml-11">Address challenges too big for individual nations through radical reforms and shifting appropriate competencies to the EU level.</p>
    </div>
